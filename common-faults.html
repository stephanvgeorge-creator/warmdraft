Common Boiler Faults

Basic troubleshooting tips to help you identify common heating system problems.

Typical Issues Homeowners Experience

Low Water Pressure

Check your boiler manual for the filling loop valve and top up the pressure to 1.0–1.5 bar. If unsure, contact us.

No Heating or Hot Water

This could indicate a problem with the thermostat, motorised valve, or pilot light. Try resetting your boiler before calling an engineer.

Radiators Not Heating Evenly

Air in the system may need bleeding. Turn off heating, allow to cool, then release air using a radiator key until water appears.

Boiler Keeps Switching Off

This can be caused by low pressure, thermostat issues, or lack of water flow. If it persists, book a visit.

⚠️ If you are not confident performing these checks, please book a qualified engineer.
