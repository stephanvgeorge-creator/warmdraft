Second Opinion Visits

Honest, experienced advice before you commit to major work or replacements.

Get Clarity Before You Decide

Been told you need an expensive boiler replacement or major system overhaul? Our Second Opinion Visits give you an expert and unbiased review of the diagnosis and costs before you commit to work.

What’s Included:

  • Independent fault assessment from Gas Safe registered engineers
  • Verification of quotations or previous engineer findings
  • Transparent pricing and written summary of our opinion
  • Guidance on repair vs replacement options

We’re committed to giving you peace of mind — not pressure. Our advice is purely based on safety, reliability, and long-term cost-effectiveness.

Common Scenarios

  • Boiler condemned — but unsure if it’s justified
  • High quotation for repair or replacement
  • Recurring fault after multiple repairs
  • Different opinions from different companies

WarmRight’s engineers will provide a clear explanation and transparent estimate — so you can make an informed decision with confidence.
