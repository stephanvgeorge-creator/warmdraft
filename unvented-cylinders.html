Unvented Cylinders

Expert installation, maintenance, and certification for your hot water system.

What Are Unvented Cylinders?

Unvented cylinders are sealed hot water storage systems that provide high-pressure hot water directly from the mains supply. They deliver strong flow rates and consistent pressure across multiple taps and showers — ideal for modern homes and multi-bathroom properties.

Professional Installation & Maintenance

WarmRight engineers are certified under the G3 Building Regulations to install, service, and maintain unvented hot water systems safely and in compliance with current legislation.

Our Unvented Cylinder Services Include:

  • New cylinder installation and commissioning
  • Annual servicing and safety checks
  • Expansion vessel replacement
  • Pressure relief and temperature valve testing
  • Re-pressurisation and system balancing

Benefits of Unvented Systems

  • Powerful mains pressure hot water
  • Eliminates need for loft tanks
  • Faster filling baths and better showers
  • Reduced noise and improved efficiency
  • Compatible with solar and renewable systems

Our engineers work with leading brands such as **Heatrae Sadia, Megaflo, Gledhill,** and **Telford** to ensure top-quality installations and repairs.
