Annual Boiler Servicing

Keep your heating system safe, efficient, and reliable with an annual service.

Why an Annual Service Matters

Regular servicing ensures that your boiler runs efficiently, safely, and lasts longer. At WarmRight Ltd, our certified engineers perform a full inspection and cleaning of all major components, identifying early signs of potential issues before they become costly repairs.

Our Annual Service Includes

  • Full safety inspection and system performance check
  • Flue analysis and combustion testing
  • Cleaning of burners, heat exchangers, and fans
  • Gas rate and pressure verification
  • Visual leak inspection and control testing
  • Official Gas Safety Record upon completion

We recommend booking your annual service between spring and early autumn, to ensure your heating system is ready for the colder months ahead.
