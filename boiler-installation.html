Boiler Installation

Efficient, professional installations tailored to your home and budget.

New Boilers Fitted by Certified Experts

At WarmRight Ltd, we install modern, energy-efficient boilers designed to reduce your energy bills and carbon footprint. Whether you're upgrading an old system or fitting a brand-new one, our Gas Safe engineers ensure safe, compliant, and long-lasting results.

Our Installation Process

  • Free home survey and consultation
  • Professional installation by Gas Safe engineers
  • System testing and efficiency optimisation
  • Manufacturer-backed warranties
  • Old boiler and waste removal

Brands We Trust

We work with all major boiler brands including Ideal, Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, and Baxi. Our team will help you choose the most efficient model for your home and budget.

Flexible Options

We offer competitive pricing, finance options, and extended warranties on selected models. Our focus is on value, reliability, and efficiency for every customer.
