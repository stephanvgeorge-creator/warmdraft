Landlord’s Gas Safety Certificates (CP12)

Keeping your tenants safe and your properties compliant.

Your Legal Responsibility, Our Expert Service

As a landlord, it’s a legal requirement to ensure all gas appliances, flues, and pipework in your rented property are checked annually by a Gas Safe registered engineer.

Our CP12 Service Includes:

  • Annual inspection of all gas appliances
  • Safety testing and certification (CP12)
  • Gas tightness testing and flue analysis
  • Digital or printed certification for your records
  • Reminder service for renewal dates

Why Choose WarmRight?

We make compliance simple and affordable. Our engineers are Gas Safe registered, reliable, and experienced with all major appliance types. We work with landlords, letting agents, and estate management companies across Kent, South East London, and East Sussex.

Multi-Property Management

We offer tailored packages for landlords or agencies managing multiple properties. Certificates can be issued electronically and stored for your convenience.
