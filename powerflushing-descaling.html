Powerflushing & Descaling

Restore efficiency and extend the life of your heating system.

What is Powerflushing?

Over time, central heating systems accumulate sludge, rust, and debris that reduce efficiency and cause cold spots in radiators. Powerflushing uses a high-flow pump and specialized chemicals to thoroughly clean your system and remove contaminants, restoring heat output and performance.

Signs You May Need a Powerflush

  • Cold spots on radiators
  • Noisy boiler or circulation pump
  • Discoloured water when bleeding radiators
  • Frequent system breakdowns
  • Boiler overheating or cutting out

Descaling Services

For homes in hard water areas, limescale can build up within heat exchangers and pipes, reducing water flow and efficiency. Our descaling service safely removes these deposits, preventing long-term damage and improving system reliability.

Benefits of Powerflushing & Descaling

  • Improved heating efficiency and reduced energy bills
  • Faster heat-up times and quieter operation
  • Prolonged boiler lifespan
  • Compliance with manufacturer warranty requirements
